modeproperty 
mode:String

Sets/Returns the BRFMode to use.

Valid modes are:

BRFMode.FACE_DETECTION: Face detection only. Always returns a rectangle, where a face was detected.

BRFMode.FACE_TRACKING: Detailed face analysis. First detects a face rectangle. Then analyses the found face to find its features.

BRFMode.POINT_TRACKING: No face detection/tracking. Frame to frame tracking of points.

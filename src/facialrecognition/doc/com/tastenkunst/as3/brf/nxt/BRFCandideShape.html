BRFCandideShape, a Candide 3 model implementation. This model is transformed according to the BRFFaceShape results and includes scale, translationX, translationY, rotationX, rotationY, rotationZ as well as the Candide 3 model vertices and triangles. 6 AcionsUnits for brows, mouth and jar are also implemented.

This model is used to update 3D engines.
